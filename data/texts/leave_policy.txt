Company Leave Policy
====================

1. Annual Leave
---------------
All full-time employees are entitled to 21 days of paid annual leave per calendar year. Leave accrues monthly at a rate of 1.75 days per month. Unused leave may be carried over to the next year up to a maximum of 5 days; any excess is forfeited on December 31st.

2. Sick Leave
-------------
Employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave per year. A medical certificate is required for sick leave exceeding 2 consecutive days. Sick leave does not carry over to the next year.

3. Parental Leave
-----------------
Primary caregivers are entitled to 16 weeks of paid parental leave. Secondary caregivers are entitled to 4 weeks of paid parental leave. Parental leave must be taken within 12 months of the child's birth or adoption date.

4. Requesting Leave
-------------------
Leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least 5 business days in advance for planned leave. Emergency leave should be reported to your manager as soon as possible, with formal documentation submitted within 3 business days of returning to work.

5. Public Holidays
------------------
The company observes 12 public holidays per year, as published in the annual holiday calendar on the intranet. Employees required to work on a public holiday receive either a day in lieu or 1.5x pay, at the employee's choice.

6. Unpaid Leave
---------------
Employees with more than 2 years of tenure may request up to 30 days of unpaid leave per year, subject to manager approval. Unpaid leave requests must be submitted at least 30 days in advance.
